Our History

A journey of vision, strategy, and disciplined execution across energy, mining, and financial technology.

BHAI Holdings LLC was established in 2020 to formalize and scale a portfolio of ventures spanning energy, mining, and financial technology. What began as independent projects across multiple sectors evolved into a unified holdings company with strategic oversight and disciplined capital allocation.

The company represents the convergence of complementary expertise: energy and mining operations, institutional banking and technology, and blockchain innovation. This combination enables BHAI Holdings to execute at both enterprise scale in traditional sectors and cutting-edge development in emerging technologies.

Timeline

2010

Oil & Gas Operations Begin

Strategic entry into oil and gas exploration. Initial investments focused on proven reserves and development opportunities in domestic markets, establishing the foundation for BHAI's energy portfolio.

2017

Cryptocurrency Trading & Blockchain Expertise

Entry into cryptocurrency markets and blockchain technology. This marked the beginning of fintech operations that would evolve into proprietary trading platforms and financial technology infrastructure, combining traditional finance expertise with emerging digital asset innovation.

2018

Gold Mining Expansion

Diversification into precious metals mining with strategic investments in gold exploration and development projects. Focus on assets with proven geological potential and NI 43-101 compliant resources, expanding the portfolio beyond energy.

2020

BHAI Holdings LLC Established

Formalization of the holdings company structure. Independent ventures across energy, mining, and emerging fintech operations unified under BHAI Holdings LLC, creating a diversified platform with strategic oversight, disciplined capital allocation, and institutional-grade operational capabilities.

Founding
2022

Fintech Expansion: GloFi Launch

Launch of GloFi, a next-generation financial technology platform addressing the $100 trillion interoperability problem. Built to bridge blockchain payment infrastructure with traditional finance, enabling seamless cross-platform asset management and financial services integration for consumers and institutions.

2023

RSVIP Platform Launch

Introduction of RSVIP, a premium event management and networking platform for high-value communities. Streamlines exclusive events with intelligent automation and VIP experience management, expanding the fintech portfolio into event technology.

2024

MX1 Platform & Energy Milestone

Launch of MX1, an AI-powered cryptocurrency trading platform featuring Mariah™—13 specialized AI agents operating 24/7. Simultaneously achieved major energy portfolio milestone with NI 51-101 compliant reserves report confirming 2.5 million barrels of proved oil reserves, demonstrating technical capability across both traditional and emerging asset classes.

Achievement
2025

$1B+ Trading Volume & Dubai Expansion

MX1 platform processed over $1 billion in trading volume, validating the fintech strategy. Simultaneously expanded operations to Dubai, UAE, establishing a presence in the Middle East's fastest-growing financial hub.
