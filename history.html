Our History

A journey of vision, strategy, and disciplined execution across energy, mining, and financial technology.

BHAI Holdings represents the culmination of over a decade of strategic investments and operational expertise across multiple sectors. The story begins with three accomplished professionals, each bringing deep domain expertise: Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth built successful ventures in oil & gas and gold mining, while Maaz Memon developed extensive experience in banking and financial technology over 15 years.

In 2020, these independent ventures and expertise were unified under BHAI Holdings LLC, creating a diversified platform to manage and scale their portfolio of wholly-owned subsidiaries and strategic partnerships across energy, mining, and fintech.

Founded By
Jonathan J. Ferrucci, John Werth & Maaz Memon

Timeline

2010

Oil & Gas Origins

Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth established strategic oil and gas exploration ventures. Initial investments focused on proven reserves and development opportunities in domestic markets, building the foundation for BHAI's energy portfolio.

2017

Fintech Expansion

Leveraging Maaz Memon's 15 years of banking and financial technology expertise, development began on proprietary trading platforms and financial services infrastructure. These ventures would evolve into MX1, GloFi, and RSVIP—transforming traditional finance through technology.

2018

Gold Mining Entry

Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth expanded their energy expertise into precious metals mining with strategic investments in gold exploration and development projects. Focus on assets with proven geological potential and NI 43-101 compliant resources.

2020

BHAI Holdings LLC Established

Jonathan J. Ferrucci, John Werth, and Maaz Memon unified their respective ventures under BHAI Holdings LLC. This consolidation brought together deep expertise in energy, mining, and financial technology, creating a diversified platform with strategic oversight and disciplined capital allocation.
