Our History

A journey of vision, strategy, and disciplined execution across energy, mining, and financial technology.

BHAI Holdings represents the culmination of over a decade of strategic investments and operational expertise across multiple sectors. The story begins with three accomplished professionals: Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth built successful ventures in oil & gas and gold mining since 2010, while Maaz Memon developed an exceptional 14-year career in traditional banking and technology—working with OKTA, Federal Reserve Bank projects, leading HSBC's $450M Global Wallet transformation program, and consulting for Expedia to create their first multi-currency payment platform.

Maaz's journey from India to New York City included groundbreaking achievements: becoming the first international student elected to the SUNY Council Board of Trustees, managing a $4M+ student budget, and earning his MBA from NYU Stern School of Business. He became a recognized voice in the global blockchain and Web3 community, speaking at Austin Consensus, Bitcoin Conference, NFT NYC, NFT Miami, and the Metaverse Summit in Paris. In 2017, the three founders discovered a shared interest in cryptocurrency trading. This partnership evolved into multiple fintech ventures—GloFi (2022), RSVIP (2023), and MX1 (2024)—combining Jonathan and John's energy and mining expertise with Maaz's institutional banking experience and blockchain innovation credentials.

In 2020, they formalized their collective vision under BHAI Holdings LLC, creating a unified platform that brings enterprise-scale technology capabilities to traditional asset sectors.

Timeline

2010

Oil & Gas Origins

Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth established strategic oil and gas exploration ventures. Initial investments focused on proven reserves and development opportunities in domestic markets, building the foundation for BHAI's energy portfolio.

2017

Blockchain & Cryptocurrency Trading Begins

Jonathan J. Ferrucci, John Werth, and Maaz Memon began trading cryptocurrency together. Maaz brought deep blockchain expertise, having become a recognized speaker at major industry events including Austin Consensus, Bitcoin Conference, NFT NYC, NFT Miami, and the Metaverse Summit in Paris. This collaboration combined energy/mining market expertise with institutional finance and emerging technology innovation, laying the groundwork for future fintech ventures.

2018

Gold Mining Entry

Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth expanded their energy expertise into precious metals mining with strategic investments in gold exploration and development projects. Focus on assets with proven geological potential and NI 43-101 compliant resources.

2020

BHAI Holdings LLC Established

Jonathan J. Ferrucci, John Werth, and Maaz Memon unified their respective ventures under BHAI Holdings LLC. This consolidation brought together deep expertise in energy, mining, and financial technology, creating a diversified platform with strategic oversight and disciplined capital allocation.

Founding
2022

Fintech Expansion: GloFi

Maaz Memon founded GloFi after recognizing the gap between emerging blockchain technology and traditional financial systems through his 14 years building projects for major institutions including OKTA, Federal Reserve Bank, HSBC ($450M transformation program), and Expedia (first multi-currency payment platform). He assembled a talented team to build infrastructure seamlessly connecting blockchain payment systems with traditional finance, addressing the $100 trillion interoperability problem and enabling everyday consumers to utilize their digital and traditional assets to full potential.

2023

RSVIP Platform Launch

Maaz Memon and Jonathan J. Ferrucci co-founded RSVIP, a premium event management and networking platform for high-value communities. The platform streamlines exclusive events with intelligent automation and VIP experience management.

2024

MX1 Trading Platform & 2.5M Barrels Proved

Jonathan J. Ferrucci and John Werth founded MX1, an AI-powered cryptocurrency trading platform featuring Mariah™—13 specialized AI agents working 24/7. Simultaneously achieved major energy milestone with NI 51-101 compliant reserves report confirming 2.5 million barrels of proved oil reserves.

MX1 platform processed over $1 billion in trading volume, validating the fintech strategy. Simultaneously expanded operations to Dubai, UAE, establishing a presence in the Middle East's fastest-growing financial hub.
