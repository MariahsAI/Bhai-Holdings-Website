John Werth

Co-Founder

Overview

John Werth is a co-founder of BHAI Holdings with extensive experience in energy and mining operations. Since 2010, he has been instrumental in building strategic investments in oil & gas exploration and gold mining, focusing on assets with proven reserves and strong development potential.

Energy & Mining Expertise

  • Co-founded NorthEast Oil & Gas operations (2010)
  • Strategic investments in gold mining projects (2018)
  • Development of 2.5M barrel proved reserves (NI 51-101)
  • Focus on sustainable extraction and proven geological formations

Fintech Ventures

  • MX1 (2024): Co-founded AI-powered cryptocurrency trading platform with Jonathan Ferrucci
  • Cryptocurrency Trading: Active since 2017
  • Strategic advisor for fintech operations at BHAI Holdings

Leadership at BHAI Holdings

As co-founder, John brings deep operational expertise in traditional energy and mining sectors, combined with forward-thinking innovation in cryptocurrency and AI-powered trading systems.
