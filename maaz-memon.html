Maaz Memon

Co-Founder & Strategic Advisor

Overview

Maaz Memon is a serial entrepreneur, angel investor, and recognized voice in the global blockchain and Web3 community with 14 years of experience in traditional banking and technology. His career spans work with Fortune 100 companies including OKTA, Federal Reserve Bank partnerships, HSBC's $450M Global Wallet transformation program, and consulting for Expedia to create their first multi-currency payment platform.

His journey from India to New York City includes groundbreaking achievements in both education and entrepreneurship, positioning him at the intersection of institutional finance and blockchain innovation.

Education & Leadership

Maaz relocated to the United States to pursue undergraduate studies in accounting and finance, where he made history by becoming the first international student elected to the SUNY Council Board of Trustees. He served as Student Association Treasurer, managing a budget exceeding $4 million and demonstrating exceptional financial stewardship.

He continued his academic journey at NYU Stern School of Business, earning his MBA while working full-time as a management consultant for a top-tier global financial institution.
